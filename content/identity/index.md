The name **European Dream**, its logo(s), and associated visual identity elements (collectively, the **European Dream Identity**) are distinctive signs identifying the European Dream initiative.

They are **not** covered by the open licences used elsewhere on this website.

---

## Ownership

The European Dream Identity is owned by **European Dream**.

Unless explicitly stated otherwise:
- the name *European Dream*,
- all current and future logo versions,
- and the associated visual identity elements

are protected by applicable intellectual property laws.

---

## Licensing status

The European Dream Identity is **not licensed** under:
- the European Union Public License (EUPL), nor
- the Creative Commons Attribution–ShareAlike licence (CC BY-SA).

No rights of reuse, modification, or redistribution are granted by default.

---

## Permitted use (nominative use)

Nominative use is permitted.

This includes:
- factual reference to the European Dream initiative,
- citation of the project name in academic, journalistic, or informational contexts,
- references required to comply with attribution obligations for licensed content.

Such use must not:
- suggest official affiliation, endorsement, or representation,
- create confusion as to the origin or governance of a project or activity.

---

## Prohibited use

Without prior written authorisation, the European Dream Identity may not be used:
- to represent another organisation, project, or initiative,
- in fundraising, advocacy, or governance activities,
- in a way that could reasonably cause confusion with the official initiative.

Forks or derivative projects based on European Dream’s open code or content
must use a **different name and visual identity**.

---

## Relationship with open licences

European Dream publishes:
- software under the **[European Union Public License (EUPL)](https://eupl.eu)**,
- editorial content under the **[Creative Commons Attribution–ShareAlike (CC BY-SA)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/)** licence.

These licences apply **only** to the respective works and **do not extend**
to the European Dream Identity.
